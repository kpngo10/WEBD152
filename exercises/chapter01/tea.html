Review of Jasmine Green Tea

I love green tea for various reasons: it's fragrant, light, pleasant, and has a chockful of antioxidant benefits. Green tea is caffeinated, but the content is nowhere as heavy as coffee. I highly recommend for anyone looking to reduce their caffeine intake.

However, I understand jasmine isn't for everyone because of its fragrance. So if you want to explore green tea, there's plenty of options: matcha (finely ground powder), gyokuro (grown under the shade), sencha (grown under full sun), genmaicha (brown rice), oolong (Chinese roasted green tea), houjicha (Japanese roasted green tea).

There's so much to talk about tea and I can go on forever. It's so good for your health. I think everyone should have a cup of tea daily for their inner peace.
